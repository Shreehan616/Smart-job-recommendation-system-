Our Solution to Skill-Job Mismatch

Instead of asking "Which job do you want?", we ask "What do you know, and what should you learn next to get a job?"

According to PLFS 2022–23, nearly 16% of rural graduates (aged 20–24) are unemployed, mainly due to a mismatch between their skills and industry requirements. Our AI platform addresses this by:

  • Providing skill-based job matching with clear gap identification
  • Offering targeted learning guidance aligned to market demand
  • Delivering region-aware recommendations for rural job seekers
  • Improving job readiness and reducing blind applications
